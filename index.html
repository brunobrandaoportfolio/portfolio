about me

Hi!
I'm Bruno.

This page serves as my personal portfolio where I'll showcase various projects I worked on, from Brand Identities to Photography or even books. Although I practice Photography, my preferred area of work is Graphic Design, I don't specialize in any specific type of Graphic Design, being open to all kinds of projects, and always willing to learn more.
