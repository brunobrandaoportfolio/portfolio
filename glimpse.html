A Glimpse of Divinity

  • Photography
  • | Art Direction

Based on Greek mythology, this series of photographic portraits aims to try to channel the facets and personalities of three Greek gods:
Aphrodite, goddess of love, beauty, lust, pleasure, and passion.
Artemis, goddess of hunting and nature.
Dionysus, god of wine, pleasure, festivity, and madness.
