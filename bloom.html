Bloom Flower Shop

Visual identity and website design concept for a fictional florist. The logo was inspired by the amaryllis flower, with the distinct shape of its petals and anthers. A secondary logo was also created in the shape of a daisy flower to be used in specific situations where the main logo may not be entirely effective. Additionally, a website design was developed with two versions, for desktop and smartphone.
